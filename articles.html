About these articles

Welcome — this collection explores how the lordship of Jesus shapes life, work, culture and community. These pieces are written to help you think about faith and influence, and to provide original content for visitors to Dominion Games. If you’re using a sister site for longer-form content, these pages can link there and bring traffic across.

Click any article below to read in full. Each article includes scripture references and practical application you can use on your site or share with others.

1. Jesus is Lord of All

An accessible, scripturally grounded piece about Christ's universal lordship and why it matters for believers and culture.

2. The Ever-Expanding Kingdom

Explores biblical images from Daniel and Isaiah and Jesus’ parables that show how God's kingdom grows and transforms from within.

3. Faith in Everyday Work

The shoemaker story (Martin Luther) and the theology of vocation: how excellent, faithful work is a witness to the world.

4. Leaven in the World

A deep dive into the parable of the leaven and practical ways believers can influence families, communities and culture.

5. Why Dominion Matters

Ties the themes together and explains the vision behind Dominion Games — stewardship, joy, creativity and faithful influence.
